Tiberian Dawn

The original that started it all.

  • Release Date: 1995
  • Genre: RTS
  • Developer: Westwood Studios
  • Publisher:
    • Virgin Interactive
    • Electronic Arts (current)

Command & Conquer (popularly referred to by its working title, Tiberian Dawn) was the first Command and Conquer game, and the starting point of the Tiberium universe. Developed by Westwood Studios in 1995, Tiberian Dawn takes place when a strange crystalline substance called Tiberium starts appearing on Earth. While the Global Defense Initiative attempts to control the situation, a faction called the Brotherhood of Nod rises and attempts to destroy GDI and embrace the "Tiberium Age". The events that follow are known as the First Tiberium War.

Downloads

Nowadays, since EA released Tiberian Dawn for free, the are multiple options of downloading the game.

  • Nyerguds

    A community member named Nyerguds managed to compile all the resources and make a community patch. The base game includes online and LAN multiplayer, singleplayer (expansion included) and music, among other things. There are also optional cutscenes and many more stuff available for download.

  • CnCNet

    CnCNet is an add-on that fixes and modernizes multiplayer capabilities. It includes a lobby, friend system, plethora of maps and all-around a full-fledged and modernized multiplayer experience.

    This package is optimized for multiplayer, managing to pack the base game with multiplayer in a roughly 30 MB download. It's also available for OSX and Linux and you can also download the full version here.
